Featured Artists

Meena Devi

Madhubani Master

From a small village in Bihar, Meena Devi learned the art of Madhubani from her grandmother. Her work is celebrated for its intricate patterns and vibrant storytelling.

View Profile

Suresh Varma

Kalamkari Expert

Suresh practices the ancient art of Kalamkari, using natural dyes and a bamboo pen to create mythological narratives on cotton fabric, a skill passed down through generations.

View Profile

Ramesh Pawar

Warli Tradition-Bearer

Hailing from the Sahyadri hills of Maharashtra, Ramesh uses the simple, geometric language of Warli art to depict the rich social and cultural life of his tribe.

View Profile

Bhuri Bai

Pithora Pioneer

A celebrated artist from the Bhil community, Bhuri Bai was one of the first to transfer the ritualistic Pithora paintings from walls to paper and canvas, bringing it to a global audience.

View Profile

Priya Rajan

Tanjore Virtuoso

Priya's Tanjore paintings are renowned for their opulent gold leaf work and embedded gems. She specializes in creating divine portraits that are both classical and contemporary.

View Profile

Jangarh Singh Shyam

Gond Modernist

A visionary from the Gond tribe, Jangarh is credited with creating a new school of Indian art called 'Jangarh Kalam,' translating the rich folklore of his people onto modern mediums.
